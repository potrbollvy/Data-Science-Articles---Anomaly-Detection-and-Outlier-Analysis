\Abstract{Once raw data has been collected and stored in a database or a dataset, the focus should shift to data cleaning and processing.  This requires testing for \textbf{soundness} and fixing \textbf{errors}, designing and implementing strategies to deal with \textbf{missing values} and \textbf{outlying/influential observations}, as well as low-level \textbf{exploratory data analysis} and \textbf{visualisation} to determine what \textbf{data transformations} and \textbf{dimension reduction} approaches will be needed in the final analysis. In this report, we  establish the essential elements of data cleaning and of data processing.
