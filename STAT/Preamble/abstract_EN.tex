\Abstract{Loosely speaking, a \textbf{statistic} is any function of a sample from the distribution of a random variable; statistics aim to extract information from an observed sample to summarise the essential features of a dataset. In this (far too) brief tour of a far-reaching and ubiquitous subject, we review ten areas of particular interest for analysts and consultants.\par\smallskip \
